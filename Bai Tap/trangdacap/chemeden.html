Black Sesame Soup

Introduce

Did you know that black sesame is known to
help with indigestion? It's no wonder that this dessert soup
iscommonly ordered at East-Asian restaurants. It mainly
consists of three ingredients—black sesame seeds, rice and
water—but sugar is usually added. Make sure to rinse your
mouth outafter though, because black residue may
stick to lips and teeth!
