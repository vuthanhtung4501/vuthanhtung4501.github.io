Dragon Beard Candy

Introduce

Dragon Beard Candy is an Asian
dessert with similarities to cotton candy.
First introduced in China, it spread
in popularity and even became a coveted
treat for Korean royalties. Made of
sugar and maltose syrup and sometimes
stuffed with ingredients such as coconut
and peanuts,chefs are trained for
months, even years, to perfect this dessert.
